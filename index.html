About Me

This is Tanvir Mazharul, currently studying at LAB University of Applied Sciences. From my childhood, I was always eager to be familiar with new technologies. This is why I have a keen interest in IT and IT-related staff.

Besides my study, I was actively involved in charity work as well. I worked for one of the Bangladeshi top renowned NPO organizations. I worked in the education sector for underprivileged children over there.

Whenever I’ve got extra time on my hand, I spend that time learning about new technologies, and also I do love online gaming.

Though I’ve done a few projects related to E-Commerce websites, School Management System previously but I’m passionate about online gaming and day by day the demand for gaming is increasing. Thus after preparing myself for the new challenges of the gaming industry, I’ll try to implement my knowledge in the gaming industry by developing new games.

After a few years, I would like to see myself as an entrepreneur in the IT industry.
